What is CYOAG?

CYOAG stands for Choose Your Own Adventure Game. The concept is not new; Steve, the CYOAG developer, borrowed the Choose Your Own Adventure idea from the books that many of those who grew up in his generation treasured as part of their childhoods. The basic idea behind these books was that you would be presented with very short chapters for context; then you'd get to pick from a handful of options that would lead to different page numbers, ultimately resulting in different paths through a story that always started the same, but had many possible trajectories and endings. Steve loved reading these Choose Your Own Adventure books and discovering the variable outcomes that could be reached.

Now, later in life, Steve has come to love writing as much as he loves reading. Having grown into a career in software development, and remembering his love of the famous Choose Your Own Adventure series of books, he decided it would be a great expression of his admiration, and a fantastic potential side project, to build a website that combines all of these loves, memories, and nostalgia into one passion project. CYOAG is, thus, a web app that proposes to let users read and create content for a Choose Your Own Adventure style story creation environment online. CYOAG is not the first to try this idea in an online format, either, but Steve hopes his rendition will demonstrate superior quality due to his dedication and passion for the project.

Steve has been asked many times, what makes CYOAG a "game." True, CYOAG is not competitive, although given chapter contributions can be voted on, and surpass each other in that respect. But not every game is, or needs to be, competitive! Think of CYOAG as a collaborative effort, a game in which everyone works together toward the end of creating the best, most riveting, interesting, rewarding story reading and writing experience possible!
