Steve is a software developer and business IT consultant by trade. He works for Pariveda Solutions, an excellent company. It is great, he likes it.

In May of 2016, Steve graduated as an M.S. in Computer Science with departmental honors from Cal State Northridge. During the program, Steve was offered over a year's worth of extensions to, and won awards for his work at, an internship with Boston Scientific. That was great, too.

While all that was going on, Steve also worked on side projects to build his skills toward a career as a professional software engineer. These include CYOAG, the site you're visiting now, as well as How Many Drinks, a simple website to help beer lovers keep their drinking habits in perspective (also hosted on GitHub).

Steve also enjoys sci-fi and fantasy in print, film, TV, video games, and other formats; playing Ultimate Frisbee; riding his motorcycle; playing guitar (used to, anyway); dealing semi-professionally in puns; and working on his second novel (due some time in 2053 at this rate if he is lucky).

If you're interested in following or contacting Steve, you can reach him on his oft neglected (but always monitored) Twitter account, @tknkldfklty.
